About Me

Hi my name is Jack Igo and I’m a 16 year old photographer based in Phoenix. My passion for photography started during the pandemic. I’m super lucky to have parents who have supported me by traveling all over the Western US in search of awesome landscapes. And I’ve been blown away by the kindness of other photographers I’ve met - helping me with tips, advice and encouragement along the way. When I’m not shooting or editing, you’ll probably find me planning my next travel adventure. I do put down the camera for a few other hobbies - backpacking, skiing and chess. I hope my love of photography shows through in my work and I’m available for creative projects including photography, drone work and video editing.
